🏰 What is Merge Archers?

Merge Archers — Kingdom Defense is a free browser wall-defense game. The horde marches on the kingdom wall; you deploy royal archers on the rampart, merge them into higher ranks, buy upgrades between waves and hold the line as long as you can. When the wall falls, your score hits the live global leaderboard.

🏹 How to play

  1. Recruit an archer onto an empty rampart slot behind the wall.
  2. Archers automatically loose arrows at raiders marching in from the right.
  3. Drag an archer onto another of the same rank to merge & promote it (Recruit → Legendary), or onto an empty slot to reposition.
  4. Between waves, spend gold at Merchant's Rest: restock Catapult & Frost, or buy permanent upgrades — hover an icon to see what it does.
  5. Chain kills fast for a combo multiplier, and watch for criticals.
  6. Every 5th wave sends a boss: the Warlord, the enraging Berserker, or the minion-conjuring Summoner.
